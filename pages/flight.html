Flight

Flight Training

I love being a pilot. I started flying in 2019, and despite the challenges thrown at me in 2020 due to the global pandemic, I did not give up on my dreams. Several pilots and aerospace professionals were hit hard by the recession that followed the pandemic and lost their jobs. However, that doesn't stop the thrilling sensation that happens when I lift up the airplane nose during takeoff. COVID-19 wouldn’t stop the breathtaking view of cruising above a blanket of clouds. No amount of doubt surrounding the fact that I am an African American woman pilot would stop me from remaining courageous and continuing my pursuit of flying. I love being in the air - not just because of invisible prestige that comes from having control in the air but also from one of my favorite quotes, said by Bessie Coleman: “The air is the only place free from prejudice”.
